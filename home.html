A strange invitation to an office in London. A malfunctioning lift. And one moment that changes everything.

Just off an ordinary street in Aldgate, something is hidden in plain sight. There’s an underworld of crime that we don’t see. Occasionally, we hear about it on the news. A confidential document left on public transport. A flight gets diverted. A diplomat disappears without a trace.

Trapped together in an elevator, you and your friends must descend into the dark underbelly of modern day espionage to lift the lid on a centuries old secret. But you’ll have to act fast if you’re going to prevent the drop.
