The Drop is taking place at 55 Aldgate High Street, London, EC3N 1AL. The venue is opposite Aldgate underground station. Aldgate station is on the Circle and Metropolitan lines, in Travelcard Zone 1.

Head over to our website and click the 'book tickets' button. Then, choose the number of people you'll have in your team, the time you'd like to play, and the date that works for you. After that, you will be asked to fill out your details and confirm your booking. Need to swap the date or time? No problem! Up to 48 hours before your planned session, you can reschedule your time or date using the 'reschedule' button on the email confirmation you are sent immediately after booking. We also sell gift vouchers at a variety of prices, which you can use to pay for your session, or gift to friends and family.

Unfortunately, all bookings are final and we cannot offer refunds or cancellations. However, you are able to exchange your session to a different date or time using the 'reschedule' button on the confirmation email. This is subject to availability, and providing the request is made at least 48 hours in advance of the session you have booked.

Due to the nature of The Drop, unfortunately we have a No Latecomers policy, so you must arrive ready to begin at the time stated on your ticket. If you are late, we will not be able to refund or reschedule your booking.
